Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a classic and delicious Indian dish known for its rich and creamy tomato-based sauce. Here's a brief overview of the recipe:

Ingredients
  • 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 cup tomato puree or crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (coriander) leaves for garnish
Steps

  1. In a large pan or skillet, heat the vegetable oil or ghee over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they become translucent.

  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pan. Cook for another minute until the raw smell disappears.

  3. Add the cumin, garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder to the pan. Stir well to coat the onions, garlic, and ginger with the spices.

  4. Add the tomato puree or crushed tomatoes to the pan and mix everything together. Allow the sauce to simmer for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.

  5. Stir in the boneless chicken pieces, coating them well with the sauce. Cover the pan and let the chicken cook in the sauce for about 10-15 minutes or until it is fully cooked and tender.

  6. Lower the heat and add the heavy cream, butter, sugar, and salt to the pan. Stir everything together and let it simmer for a few more minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. If you prefer a thinner consistency, you can add a bit of water or chicken broth.

  8. Garnish the butter chicken with fresh cilantro leaves before serving.
