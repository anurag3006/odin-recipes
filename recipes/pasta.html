Pasta

Ingredients
  • 250 grams dried pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne, fettuccine)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for added spice)
  • 1 can (400 grams) crushed tomatoes or tomato passata
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish
Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside, reserving some pasta water.

  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using) and saut√© for about a minute until the garlic becomes fragrant.

  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes or tomato passata, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Stir well, and let the sauce simmer on low heat for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.

  4. If the sauce is too thick, you can add some of the reserved pasta water to achieve your desired consistency.

  5. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce, coating it evenly. Allow the pasta to absorb some of the sauce while gently stirring.

  6. Serve the pasta in individual dishes and garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves, if desired.
